Major new update to the titrate page: You now can manually modify the recommended titration amount calculated. Additionally, we have some interesting changes in the pipes for the titration page based on some groundbreaking research!

Announcements

Available on mobile devices!

The iPhone, Android devices, Palm's (discontinued) WebOS, as well as practically any other smartphones that have modern browsers with javascript support work with MyInsul.in. To access, simply go to http://MyInsul.in/ on your device!

Furthermore, a tablet-based version is being developed. Unfortunately, for the time being, you may either be brought to the fullsize website or mobile website.

Announcements:

Designed for use at Legacy Health

Our program is designed, at this point, for use in the Legacy Health system. All guidelines and equations used by MyInsul.in, pertain to Legacy Health.

However, a patient isn't different from one hospital to another... Feel free to adopt to the program now before we move towards generalized usage!
