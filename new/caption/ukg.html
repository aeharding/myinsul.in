The Units/kg ratio represents a relationship between total daily units of insulin (TDD) to the patient's weight.

Units/KG ratio:

The units/kg ratio can be estimated from a variety of factors:

0.3 units/kg body weight:
  • Underweight
  • Older age
  • Hemodialysis
0.4 units/kg body weight:
  • Normal body habitus: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Normal weight
0.5 units/kg body weight:
  • Overweight (BMI 25-29.9)
