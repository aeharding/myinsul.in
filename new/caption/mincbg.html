This is the target for your patient's supplemental insulin scale, and is the minimum CBG expected to be seen.
This plus your increment will be a base of zero units perscribed.

100 is the usual base for the scale, and in most cases should stay this.
In a pregnant patient, this base should probably be lower to account for a lower goal BG of 80 units.
Alternatively, a patient in critical care may need a higher minimum CBG.
