3 meals per day: The patient is on a diabetic diet (fed 3 times per day).

Continuous tube feed: Insulin is distributed evenly through the day through the tube feed. Measured in 6 hour increments, A continous tube feed is automatically divided into a 40%-basal 60%-bolus regimen instead of 50%/50%.

Bolus tube feed: Few patients are on this feeding type. When clicked, an option for the amount of feeding times for the day will be shown.
